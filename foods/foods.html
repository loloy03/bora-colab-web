5 Restaurants in Boracay & What to Eat There

Boracay, a small island off the coast of the Philippines, has risen to prominence as a popular tourist destination due to its resorts, beaches, and picturesque features. Watersports, panoramic views, scuba diving, and coral reefs are some of the most popular tourist attractions. Boracay's cuisine is rarely recognized, despite the fact that it offers some of the best in class Italian, Asian, and Chinese dishes that are similar to its magnificent beaches and scenic splendor.

1. Beer at Crafty's Rooftop Bar

Crafty's Rooftop Bar is the ideal spot for a chilled beer and some light appetizers while watching a magnificent sunset. Crafty's specializes on a large range of wine, cocktails, and vodka, in addition to all types of beer. If you're out for a family supper, what better location to go than Crafty's restaurant, which is one story below. Burgers, soups, pizza, spaghetti, Filipino/ Asian dishes, grilled meats and fish, and other menu items are must-trys.
